Aim one

Popularisation of tennis(healthy lifestyle).The first aim is to make tennis popular worldwide starting from the countries of Northern Europe and considering all the economic benefits it could bring to the Northern European countries which are Ireland, Denmark, Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, Finland, Switzerland, Norway, Iceland, United Kingdom.

Two

Make tennis more affordable. The second aim is to provide more opportunities to play tennis for people with low incomes and therefore encourage bigger amount of people to try this activity.

Three

Let people know about advantages tennis can bring. The third aim is to raise awareness that tennis can improve many areas of people’s life, such as for example health. And how does this will increase overall standard of living and lead to less money needed for investment in health and both the government budget and people’s disposable income can be saved and spent on something else.

Four

Promote tennis as competitive industry. The fourth aim is to increase the number of competitions and large-scale tournaments which therefore it is going to increase the competitiveness of NE countries worldwide and bring large profits.

Five

Show different tennis sides. The fifth aim is to increase the number of events related to tennis such as exhibitions, designed tennis clothes, meetings with the players.
